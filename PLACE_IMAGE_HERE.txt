  TRANSFER SCREEN IMAGE SETUP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To complete the transfer screen setup:

1. Rename your image file:
   transfer.jpeg → worldgate_transfer.png

2. Place it in this location:
   worldgate/textures/worldgate_transfer.png

3. The final path should be:
   /path/to/minetest/mods/worldgate/textures/worldgate_transfer.png

4. Restart your Minetest/Luanti server

THAT'S IT!

When players right-click a linked servergate beacon, they'll see
your custom portal image as a beautiful fullscreen background.
